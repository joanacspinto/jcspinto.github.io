Biography

I am currently a neuroimaging Postdoctoral Researcher at University of Oxford.
Additionally, I am also a Junior Research Fellow at Kellogg College, University of Oxford, and an Early Career Fellow of the European Society of Magnetic Resonance in Medicine and Biology.

Previously, I was at Instituto Superior Técnico, Universidade de Lisboa, where I did my PhD in Biomedical Engineering (2019).
