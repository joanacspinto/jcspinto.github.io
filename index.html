Biography

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at University of Oxford. Additionally, I am also a Junior Research Fellow at Kellogg College, University of Oxford, and an Early Career Fellow of the European Society of Magnetic Resonance in Medicine and Biology. Previously, I was at Instituto Superior Técnico, Universidade de Lisboa, where I did my PhD, MSc and BSc (Biomedical Engineering).
